## Description
Causes the mob to jump off of its mount, if it is mounted on anything.
Otherwise, does nothing. 

> **This is a no-target mechanic, and the affected entity will always be the caster**


## Attributes
> *This mechanic has no attributes*


## Examples
```yaml
  Skills:
  - dismount ~onDamaged
```
