## Description
Creates an explosion effect at the specified target. Will play the sound and the particle effects, but won't cause any damage.


## Attributes
> *This mechanic has no attributes*


## Examples
```yaml
ExplosionSkill:
  Skills:
  - fakeexplosion @self
```
