## Description
Generates the portal of the EnderDragon battle


## Attributes
| Attribute | Aliases   | Description                                                          | Default |
|-----------|-----------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|---------|
| withPortals | wp, p   | Whether to generate the portal of the EnderDragon battle             | false   |


## Examples
```yaml
  Skills:
  - enderDragonSpawnPortal @selflocation 
```
