## Description
Prints debug information regarding the Metaskill executing the mechanic and its SkillTree


## Attributes
> *This mechanic has no attributes*


## Examples
```yaml
Example1:
  Skills:
  - skill{s=Example2}

Example2:
  Skills:
  - skill{s=Example3}

Example3:
  Skills:
  - printParentTree
```
