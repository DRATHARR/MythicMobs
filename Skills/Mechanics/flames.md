## Description
Creates the flames effect at the location of the targeter. Imitates the appearance of Minecraft's mob spawner block flames and thus has a randomness-factor to it.


## Attributes
> *This mechanic has no attributes*


## Examples
```yaml
FlamesSkill:
  Skills:
  - flames @self
```

- [x] e:flames
